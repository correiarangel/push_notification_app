Principal Content

Taking Harvard’s CS50: Introduction to Computer Science course was an extremely privileged experience, as well as being fun and exciting. The quality of the teaching and the teachers is truly exceptional.

Although Brazilian teachers are also brilliant, David Malan's teaching method stands out. His examples and illustrations are extremely effective in helping students learn the content. I would like to express my gratitude to the Estudar group, which manages and makes this platform available for the course in Brazil.

I would also like to thank the professors and everyone involved in this incredible opportunity to enter this fascinating world of software development. The R Notifications system was developed as a final project for the course. If you are interested in the subject and want to explore a career as a developer, check out the links in the panel on the right for available courses.
